FCM codes: For push notifications, the App may generate unique codes to manage messaging between users. To communicate with users – sending FCM codes, friend requests, messages, and alarms. To improve the App – troubleshooting, technical support, and operational monitoring. We process your data only when there is a legitimate purpose, such as fulfilling contractual obligations, protecting vital interests, or with your consent. 3.
